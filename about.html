01 · Why we exist

Built for the healthy 95%.

India's healthcare is designed for the 5% who are already ill. The other 95% get an annual PDF, a red number with no explanation, and silence until something goes wrong.

Yello started with a simple observation from inside radiology and diagnostics: the information that could keep a family ahead of illness usually exists — scattered across labs, lost in reports nobody explains, disconnected from last year's numbers.

So we built the network we wanted for our own families: diagnostic and imaging centres held to one quality bar, reports rebuilt in plain language, a doctor to talk every result through, and one record that follows you for life — not the lab.

05 · Common questions

Asked often, answered plainly.

What is Yello?

A curated preventive health network in Hyderabad. We hold every partner centre to one quality bar, explain every report in plain language, include a doctor consult with every booking, and keep your family's health record in one place for life.

What is a health twin?

The living picture of your body that Yello builds from your test history. Every test is a calibration event — it adds a data point, updates your trends, and makes the twin sharper. You see it as a timeline, trend lines per marker, and one view per family member.

How is this different from a lab aggregator?

Aggregators sell tests. Yello sells staying well: curated centres rather than listings, programs rather than one-offs, explanations rather than PDFs, and a record that compounds in value the longer you stay. The test is the beginning of the product, not the end.

Is my health data safe?

Yes. Yello follows India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Your records belong to you — you choose who sees them, you can export them, and you can ask for them to be deleted. Records are kept ABHA-ready.

Where does Yello operate?

We're starting in Hyderabad with a small, carefully chosen network of centres, and expanding city by city. Home sample collection is available across the service area.
